Molly Prewett
=============
Molly Prewett.
She was born on October 30, 1950.
Her siblings are Fabian and Gideon.

Molly Prewett is the partner of Arthur Weasley. :doc:`Arthur and Molly<@F58900171@>` have 7 children together. They are called Bill, Charlie, Percy, George, Fred, Ron and Ginny.
