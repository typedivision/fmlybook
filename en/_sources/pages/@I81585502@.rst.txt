James Potter
============
James Potter is the son of Fleamont Potter and Euphemia (Potter).
He was born on March 27, 1960.


James Potter is the partner of Lily Evans. :doc:`James and Lily<@F10148954@>` have a child called Harry.

James died on October 31, 1981 at the age of 21 in Godric's Hollow.
