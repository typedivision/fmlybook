Harry Potter
============
Harry James Potter is the son of :doc:`James Potter and Lily Evans<@F10148954@>`.
He was born on July 31, 1980 in Godric's Hollow.


Harry Potter married Ginny Weasley. :doc:`Harry and Ginny<@F59662488@>` have 3 children together. They are called James, Albus and Lily.
