Lily Evans
==========
Lily J. Evans.
She was born on January 30, 1960.
The name of her sister is Petunia.

Lily Evans is the partner of James Potter. :doc:`James and Lily<@F10148954@>` have a child called Harry.

Lily died on October 31, 1981 at the age of 21 in Godric's Hollow.
