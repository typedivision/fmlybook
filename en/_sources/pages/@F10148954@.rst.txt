James Potter & Lily Evans
=========================
:doc:`James<@I81585502@>` Potter was born on March 27, 1960.
His parents are Fleamont Potter and Euphemia (Potter).
James died on October 31, 1981.

:doc:`Lily<@I43481114@>` J. Evans was born on January 30, 1960.
Lily died on October 31, 1981.


James and Lily have a son. He is called :doc:`Harry<@I76753198@>`.

Harry James Potter was born on July 31, 1980 in Godric's Hollow.
