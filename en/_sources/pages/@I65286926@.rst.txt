Ginny Weasley
=============
Ginevra Molly "Ginny" Weasley is the daughter of :doc:`Arthur Weasley and Molly Prewett<@F58900171@>`.
She was born on August 11, 1981.
Her siblings are Bill, Charlie, Percy, George, Fred and Ron.

Ginny Weasley married Harry Potter. :doc:`Harry and Ginny<@F59662488@>` have 3 children together. They are called James, Albus and Lily.
