Arthur Weasley
==============
Arthur Weasley is the son of Septimus Weasley and Cedrella Black.
He was born on February 6, 1950.


Arthur Weasley is the partner of Molly Prewett. :doc:`Arthur and Molly<@F58900171@>` have 7 children together. They are called Bill, Charlie, Percy, George, Fred, Ron and Ginny.
