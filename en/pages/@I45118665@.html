Molly Prewett¶

Molly Prewett. She was born on October 30, 1950. Her siblings are Fabian and Gideon.

Molly Prewett is the partner of Arthur Weasley. Arthur and Molly have 7 children together. They are called Bill, Charlie, Percy, George, Fred, Ron and Ginny.
