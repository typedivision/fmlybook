Harry Potter & Ginny Weasley¶

Harry James Potter was born on July 31, 1980. His parents are James Potter and Lily Evans.

Ginevra Molly “Ginny” Weasley was born on August 11, 1981. Her parents are Arthur Weasley and Molly Prewett.

Harry Potter and Ginny Weasley are married. Harry and Ginny have 3 children together. They are called James, Albus and Lily.

James Sirius Potter was born in 2004.

Albus Severus Potter was born in 2006.

Lily Luna Potter was born in 2008.
