Ginny Weasley¶

Ginevra Molly “Ginny” Weasley is the daughter of Arthur Weasley and Molly Prewett. She was born on August 11, 1981. Her siblings are Bill, Charlie, Percy, George, Fred and Ron.

Ginny Weasley married Harry Potter. Harry and Ginny have 3 children together. They are called James, Albus and Lily.
