Arthur Weasley¶

Arthur Weasley is the son of Septimus Weasley and Cedrella Black. He was born on February 6, 1950.

Arthur Weasley is the partner of Molly Prewett. Arthur and Molly have 7 children together. They are called Bill, Charlie, Percy, George, Fred, Ron and Ginny.
