James Potter & Lily Evans¶

James Potter was born on March 27, 1960. His parents are Fleamont Potter and Euphemia (Potter). James died on October 31, 1981.

Lily J. Evans was born on January 30, 1960. Lily died on October 31, 1981.

James and Lily have a son. He is called Harry.

Harry James Potter was born on July 31, 1980 in Godric’s Hollow.
