Lily Evans¶

Lily J. Evans. She was born on January 30, 1960. The name of her sister is Petunia.

Lily Evans is the partner of James Potter. James and Lily have a child called Harry.

Lily died on October 31, 1981 at the age of 21 in Godric’s Hollow.
